What is the Internet?

Category: Networking | Date: 26 Nov 2025

The internet is a global network of computers that are connected to each other. It allows us to send information, watch videos, chat with friends, and visit websites.

Every website you open is stored on a special computer called a server. Your device connects to these servers using the internet.
